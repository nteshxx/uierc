Image restoration is the process of recovering
an image from a degraded version.

It is the operation of taking a corrupt/noisy
image and estimating the clean, original image.

Corruption may come in many forms such as
motion blur, noise or camera mis-focus.

Restoration Methods

  • DCP
  • MIP
  • ULAP
